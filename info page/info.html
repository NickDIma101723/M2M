Section 1: Fitness and VR Gym

The spaceship has a fitness area with zero-gravity workout equipment and a VR gym offering immersive virtual workouts. Health monitoring tracks passengers' fitness and vital signs.

Section 2: Food and Nutrition

Passengers enjoy space-friendly meals, including customizable options for special diets. The food system offers a variety of healthy meals with AI-assisted suggestions for nutrition.

Section 3: Entertainment (Movies, Music, and eBooks)

The spaceship provides movies, TV shows, and documentaries for entertainment. A diverse music library and eBooks (including educational content) keep passengers engaged throughout the journey.

Extra Features

The ship offers real-time communication with Earth, sleep pods for restful sleep, and advanced medical equipment for passenger safety and health.
