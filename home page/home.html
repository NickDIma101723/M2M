Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often referred to as the "Red Planet" due to its reddish appearance, caused by iron oxide (rust) on its surface. It has a thin atmosphere primarily made of carbon dioxide and experiences extreme temperatures. Mars is known for its large volcano, Olympus Mons, and the longest canyon in the solar system, Valles Marineris. The planet has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, and has been a target for exploration due to its potential for past water and future human missions.
